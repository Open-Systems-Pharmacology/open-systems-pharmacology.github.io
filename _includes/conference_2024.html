2024 OSP COMMUNITY CONFERENCE IN BASEL, SWITZERLAND

The first OSP Community Conference will be held in Basel, Switzerland on 7-8th October, 2024. Basel is Switzerland's third-largest city and a dynamic hub for pharmaceutical, life sciences and financial companies. With a high quality of life, excellent public transportation, and diverse culinary options, Basel combines business efficiency with cultural richness. The conference will take place in the Gehry Building on the Novartis Campus, designed by Frank Gehry and completed in 2000. Its bold design and innovative use of materials make it a unique landmark on the map of Basel.
