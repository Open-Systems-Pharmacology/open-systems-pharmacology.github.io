2026 OSP COMMUNITY CONFERENCE IN Antwerp, Belgium

The third OSP Community Conference will take place in Antwerp! On 24-25th September 2026, location to be announced.​

Antwerp is a vibrant port city in northern Belgium where medieval charm meets contemporary culture, making it a compelling destination for visitors. Known as the world’s diamond capital, it boasts a rich trading history reflected in its grand architecture, from the Gothic Cathedral of Our Lady to the ornate Guild Houses lining the Grote Markt. The city is also a global fashion hub, home to cutting-edge designers and stylish boutiques, while its museums celebrate masters like Rubens alongside modern art. Lively cafés, excellent Flemish cuisine, and a scenic riverfront along the Scheldt round out Antwerp’s appeal as a city that feels both historic and forward-looking.
