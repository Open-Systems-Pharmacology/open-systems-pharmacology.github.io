2025 OSP COMMUNITY CONFERENCE IN PARIS, FRANCE

The second OSP Community Conference will take place in Paris! On 29-30th September 2025, at Sanofi: 13 Quai Jules Guesde, 94400 Vitry-sur-Seine.​

Vitry-sur-Seine, located just outside the heart of Paris, is a vibrant area known for its contemporary art scene and industrial heritage. With easy access to the city center, excellent public transportation, and Paris's renowned culture, cuisine, and history, this location offers the perfect blend of convenience and inspiration.​

The conference venue is a modern and versatile space designed to foster collaboration and innovation, setting the stage for an engaging and impactful event. We will soon announce the exciting venue for the social evening!
